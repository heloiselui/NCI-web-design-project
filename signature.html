Our Signature Cocktails!

Libations of Inspiration: Explore Our Collection of Handcrafted Cocktails

Embark on a journey through our world of libations, where each drink is a symphony of flavors meticulously crafted to captivate your senses. From timeless classics to innovative concoctions, our cocktail collection is a celebration of mixology mastery.

Cosmopolitan

Step into a world of timeless elegance with our Cosmopolitan—a dazzling blend of premium vodka, tangy cranberry, a splash of zesty orange liqueur, and a twist of freshly squeezed lime.

This iconic pink-hued libation exudes sophistication, teasing the palate with a harmonious balance of sweet and tart flavors. Served in a chilled martini glass and garnished with a flirty citrus twist, it's a chic and vibrant sip that embodies cosmopolitan charm in every glamorous drop.

Click the image to see the ingredients!

Ingredients

- 1.5 oz citrus vodka

- 1 oz Cointreau

- .5 oz lime juice

- .25 oz cranberry juice

Espresso Martini

Indulge in a seductive symphony of bold espresso, premium vodka, and a touch of coffee liqueur, shaken to perfection.

This elegant elixir balances the rich, roasted depths of freshly brewed coffee with a velvety smoothness that dances across your palate, awakening the senses with every sip. Served chilled in a martini glass, it's the sophisticated jolt you crave, a union of caffeine and cocktail craftsmanship that's simply irresistible.

Click the image to see the ingredients!

Ingredients

- 2 oz vodka

- 1.5 oz Kahlúa

- 0.5 oz sugar syrup

- 1 strong Espresso

Margarita

Embark on a journey to the sun-soaked shores of Mexico with our Margarita—a refreshing fusion of premium tequila, the tang of freshly squeezed lime juice, and a whisper of orange liqueur.

This iconic cocktail is a celebration in a glass, striking the perfect harmony between citrusy brightness and the smooth, earthy essence of tequila. Served over ice in a salt-rimmed glass, each sip is a fiesta for the senses, delivering a zesty kick that transports you to a breezy, carefree paradise.

Click the image to see the ingredients!

Ingredients

- 2 oz silver tequila

- 1 oz Cointreau

- 1 oz lime juice

- Salt for the rim

Whiskey Sour

Experience the timeless allure of a classic with our Whiskey Sour—a tantalizing blend of top-shelf bourbon, freshly squeezed lemon juice, and just a hint of sweetness.

This iconic libation marries the rich warmth of bourbon with the crisp, tangy embrace of citrus, creating a harmonious balance that's both smooth and invigorating. Served over ice with a delicate cherry garnish, each sip unveils layers of complexity, inviting you to savor the perfect fusion of sweet, sour, and the unmistakable depth of fine whiskey.

Click the image to see the ingredients!

Ingredients

- 2 oz whiskey

- 1 oz lemon juice

- 1 tsp sugar

- 1 egg white

Martini

Embrace the epitome of sophistication with our Martini—a flawless fusion of premium gin or vodka, vermouth, and a touch of artistry.

This iconic elixir, served in a chilled, stemmed glass, embodies refined simplicity. Each sip unveils a symphony of botanicals or the smooth embrace of vodka, perfectly complemented by the subtle dance of vermouth. With an optional twist of citrus or an elegant olive garnish, this classic cocktail is an ode to elegance, inviting you to savor the pure essence of indulgence in a glass.

Click the image to see the ingredients!

Ingredients

- 3 oz gin or vodka

- .5 oz dry vermouth

- Lemon peel or olive

Mint Julep

Transport yourself to the serene charm of the Southern countryside with our Mint Julep—a refreshing union of muddled fresh mint, fine bourbon, and a touch of simple syrup over crushed ice.

This iconic cocktail is a cool, invigorating sip that balances the robust character of bourbon with the invigorating essence of mint. Served in a frosty silver cup, it's a timeless concoction that whispers of lazy afternoons and southern hospitality, offering a delightful fusion of tradition and refreshment in every sip.

Ingredients

- 2 oz bourbon/p>

- 8-10 mint leaves

- .25 oz simple syrup
